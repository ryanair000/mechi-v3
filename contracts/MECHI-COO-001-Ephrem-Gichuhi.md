## 1. ENGAGEMENT & SCOPE

Lokimax Ventures is a Kenyan company operating **Mechi** as its esports division — a gaming matchmaking platform built for Kenyan Gen Z gamers. The Company hereby engages the Contractor on a **part-time/contract basis** as **Head of Operations, Platform Tester & Tournament Streaming** for the Mechi esports platform.

This is not an employment contract. The Contractor operates as an independent contractor and is responsible for their own tax obligations under Kenyan law.

---

## 2. DUTIES & RESPONSIBILITIES

The Contractor agrees to perform the following duties for the duration of this contract:

### 2.1 Platform Operations
- Manage day-to-day platform operations, including coordinating with the Mechi admin team on platform uptime, player-reported issues, and technical escalations
- Oversee user support workflows — triaging player complaints, dispute reports, and account issues through the Mechi admin panel
- Act as the escalation point between players and the development/admin team for issues unresolved at first contact

### 2.2 Platform Testing
- Serve as a **primary platform tester** for Mechi V3 and all future updates
- Test new features before they are released to the public, covering key flows including: matchmaking, queue, lobbies, tournaments, challenges, gamification (XP, MP, achievements, rank divisions), notifications, and player profiles
- Document and report all bugs, UI/UX issues, and unexpected behaviours in a clear and timely manner directly to the development team
- Perform regression testing after bug fixes to confirm issues are fully resolved
- Provide honest, player-perspective feedback on new features from the standpoint of Mechi's core Gen Z Kenyan gaming audience
- Participate in testing sessions ahead of major tournament launches to ensure the platform is stable and ready

### 2.3 Tournament Operations
- Plan, schedule, and execute all Mechi tournaments end-to-end using the platform's tournament system
- Manage the full tournament lifecycle: bracket creation, player registration and check-ins, match scheduling, dispute resolution, and prize coordination
- Communicate tournament schedules and updates to the Mechi community across all relevant channels (WhatsApp, Instagram, TikTok, in-app notifications)
- Ensure all participating players meet eligibility requirements before tournaments go live

### 2.4 Live Stream Production
- Host and commentate all Mechi tournament finals and major events live on YouTube, TikTok, and/or Twitch
- Manage personal stream setup including overlays, audio, and match timing coordination with players
- Deliver engaging, on-brand commentary that reflects Mechi's identity and resonates with Kenyan Gen Z gamers

### 2.5 Broadcast Coordination
- Coordinate with players before and during live tournaments to ensure matches are played on schedule
- Communicate any delays, bracket changes, or player forfeitures live to the audience in a professional manner
- Liaise with the admin team in real time during broadcasts to surface and resolve any platform-side issues mid-stream

### 2.6 Community Engagement During Streams
- Actively moderate the live chat during all broadcasts
- Run viewer polls, Q&As, and interactive segments to drive community participation
- Announce match winners, rank milestones, and achievement unlocks (e.g., Hat Trick streaks, Legend rank attainment, level-up moments) as they occur on the Mechi gamification system
- Drive hype around Mechi's rank divisions (Bronze → Legend) and XP/MP reward system to encourage platform signups and retention

## 3. COMPENSATION

### 3.1 Signing Bonus (One-Time)
Upon signing this contract, the Company shall pay the Contractor a **one-time upfront payment of KES 1,500 (One Thousand Five Hundred Kenya Shillings)** via M-Pesa within **48 hours** of both parties signing. This payment is non-refundable and acknowledges the Contractor's commitment to begin their duties immediately.

### 3.2 Weekly Retainer
The Company shall pay the Contractor a weekly retainer of **KES 1,000 (One Thousand Kenya Shillings)**, payable every Friday via M-Pesa or agreed mobile money transfer.

### 3.3 Tournament Revenue Share
For every tournament hosted on the Mechi platform under this contract, the Contractor shall receive **50% (fifty percent) of net tournament profits**.

"Net tournament profits" is defined as total tournament entry fees collected, minus platform processing fees, prize pool payouts, and any direct tournament costs (e.g., promotional spend) pre-agreed in writing by both parties before the tournament launches.

Payment of the tournament revenue share shall be made within **5 business days** of the tournament conclusion and prize distribution being finalized.

### 3.4 Future Increments
Both parties acknowledge that Mechi is a growing esports platform under Lokimax Ventures. As the Mechi fanbase, player base, and tournament participation grow, the Company commits to **good-faith renegotiation of compensation** — including the weekly retainer and revenue share structure — at the following milestones:

- Every **3 months** from the date of this contract, or
- When average tournament participation exceeds **100 players per event**, or
- When Mechi's registered user base reaches agreed growth thresholds

Any increments will be documented as a written addendum to this contract, signed by both parties.

## 4. CONTENT & IP OWNERSHIP

- All stream recordings, clips, and content produced in the performance of duties under this contract that feature the Mechi brand, logo, platform, or tournaments are considered **co-owned** between Lokimax Ventures and the Contractor.
- Lokimax Ventures retains the right to repurpose, distribute, and publish such content across Mechi's official channels with attribution to the Contractor.
- The Contractor may retain and publish such content on their personal channels provided Mechi and Lokimax Ventures are credited.

---

## 5. CONFIDENTIALITY

The Contractor agrees not to disclose any confidential information relating to Lokimax Ventures, Mechi's platform architecture, user data, revenue figures, bug reports, test findings, or business strategy to any third party during and for **12 months** after the termination of this contract.

---

## 6. TERM & TERMINATION

- This contract commences on the date of signing and continues for an initial period of **3 months**, renewable by mutual written agreement.
- Either party may terminate this contract with **14 days' written notice** (via WhatsApp or email constitutes valid written notice).
- In the event of termination, any outstanding tournament revenue share owed to the Contractor must be settled within 7 business days. The signing bonus is non-refundable under any termination circumstances.

---

## 7. GOVERNING LAW

This contract is governed by the laws of the **Republic of Kenya**. Any disputes shall be resolved first through good-faith negotiation, and if unresolved, through mediation in Mombasa County.
