# Mechi Rewards

Mechi gives players reward points, game redeemables, and cash prizes through bounties and tournaments.

## What Players Can Get

| Reward | What it is | How to get it |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Reward Points (RP) | Points earned inside Mechi. The wallet rate is `100 points = KSh 10`. | Play matches, complete your profile, invite players, share, stream, and join competitive events. |
| Game redeemables | CODM CP, PUBG Mobile UC, and eFootball Coins bought with RP. | Go to `Rewards > Redeemables`, choose a pack, confirm your M-Pesa number, and submit the request. |
| Cash bounties | Live challenge prizes worth KES 50, KES 100, or KES 200. | Open `Bounties`, complete the active challenge trigger, and win before it is claimed. Payout is sent to M-Pesa after approval. |
| Tournament prizes | Prize pool cash for tournament winners. | Join a tournament, play your bracket, and win the final. |

## How To Earn RP

| Action | Reward |
| --- | ---: |
| Complete your profile | 200 RP once |
| Log in today | 10 RP daily |
| Play your first match of the day | 30 RP daily |
| Win 3 in a row | 75 RP daily |
| Win 5 in a row | 150 RP weekly |
| Win 10 in a row | 400 RP weekly |
| Advance a rank tier | 100 RP per tier |
| Share from your Share page | 25 RP daily |
| Get a signup through your invite code | 300 RP per signup |
| Join through an invite and play your first match | 500 RP once |
| Watch a live stream | 5 RP per reward block, up to 20 RP per day |
| Go live for at least 10 minutes | 50 RP |

Tournament and lobby bonuses can also award RP when active, including tournament wins, runner-up finishes, top-four placements, first tournament joins, season top-ten finishes, clean best-of-3 sweeps, lobby first places, and lobby win streaks.

## Redeemable Packs

- CODM: 30 CP up to 10,800 CP.
- PUBG Mobile: 60 UC up to 8,100 UC.
- eFootball: 137 Coins up to 32,200 Coins.

Redemption requests move through pending, processing, completed, or rejected. If a redemption is rejected, the spent points can be restored by an admin.
