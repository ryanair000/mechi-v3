# Mechi Snapshot

- matchmaking and player challenges
- tournaments and hosting
- PlayMechi Online Gaming Tournament: 216-slot free online event across PUBG Mobile, CODM, and eFootball

- rewards and redeemables
- support and operator dashboards
- Android and marketing surfaces

## Visible plan framing

- `free`: limited matches and one game
- `pro`: unlimited matches, more games, tournament hosting
- `elite`: higher-tier access and reduced tournament fees

## Operating caution

- do not promise billing, rewards, payouts, or account actions without verification
- use live data paths such as Supabase helpers for current numbers
- for PlayMechi tournament facts use `skills/playmechi-tournament-ops/SKILL.md`; for live counts use `npm run ops:registrations -- --json` and the `onlineTournament` object
